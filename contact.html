Contact

Drop me a line or two

If you want or need to contact me you can easily do so by sending an email to my email account eduanlavaque@gmail.com.

If you want to request me as a freelancer please include "Freelancer: " at the start of the message title. Also please include the following info in your email:

  • Your first and last names.
  • The email to which I should send the answer to.
  • Your budget, i.e. how much you can pay me for this job.
  • The description of what you want/need.

Now, the only requirement I have in order for you to hire me as a freelancer is that you aren't a Client From Hell (no offense).

Another thing, while I can certainly guarantee that my work will be high quality, I cannot guarantee it'll be done in time if you give me a short timespan to do it in, I prefer quality over quantity in anything and everything, including my work. And I don't like time limits, at all.
